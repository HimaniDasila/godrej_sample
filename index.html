Overview

Godrej 89

At Sector 89, New Gurgaon (Opp To Godrej Air)

Godrej Zenith in Sector 89 Gurgaon is a prestigious residential project by Godrej Properties, strategically located in the heart of Gurgaon. Offering a wide range of plot sizes and configurations, from 2 BHK to 4 BHK with servant quarters, the project caters to diverse lifestyle needs. With nine towers spread across the development, residents can enjoy ample privacy and tranquility. The project's prime location near the Dwarka Expressway ensures excellent connectivity to key areas like Palam Vihar, Dwarka, and the Indira Gandhi International Airport, making it an ideal choice for homeowners. The luxurious amenities, including a lavish clubhouse, indoor and outdoor sports facilities, landscaped gardens, and a dedicated play area, elevate the living experience. With top-notch security measures and a focus on green spaces, Godrej Zenith promises a comfortable and upscale lifestyle in Gurgaon.

Godrej Properties has grabbed attention in Gurgaon's real estate by getting a big piece of land in Sector 89. This shows they're serious about making great homes. The location is near Golf Course Road Extension, a growing area with good connections. It's close to important railway stations, Gurgaon's business center, and upcoming Metro stations. This area is set to become a bustling hub with more businesses and better transport.
